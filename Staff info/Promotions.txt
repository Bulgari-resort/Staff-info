All LR-MR promotions


1 |LR | Guests: These people are the people staying at the hotel!
2 |LR | Needs training: These people have passed the application centers and are awaiting training
3 |LR | Junior staff: These people have passed training and are qualified to work at the hotel properly
4 |LR | Senior staff: These people are considered SLR and have done lots to help our hotel
5 |LR | Hotel intern: These people are the last LR to get from Senior staff to Intern you need to work hard. You take training to move to the next rank.
6 |MR| Resort supervisor: This rank is the first MR rank. These people are usually very good at the game have taken training and done shifts.
7 |MR| Server moderator: This rank is like an assistant owner to the server. These people are taked with making sure everything runs smoothly and helping the server owner.
8 |MR| Server owner: The final MR rank this rank is a very rare one this is the final promotion! These people have worked super hard to get here!
The rest are HR
Promotion requirements
1 |LR | Guests: Join the group 
2 |LR | Needs training: Pass application or have 250 points
3 |LR | Junior staff: Pass training or attend a shift with 500 points
4 |LR | Senior staff: Attend a shift with 1750 points
5 |LR | Hotel intern: Attend a shift with 5000 points
6 |MR| Resort supervisor: Do a training as an intern or have 7500 points
7 |MR| Server moderator: Attend a shift with 10000 points
8 |MR| Server owner: Attend a shift with 20000 points and work really hard in front of a HR or SHR
