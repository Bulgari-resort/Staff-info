Moderator have a lot of commands. It can be overwhelming!!!
These are the commands and their uses
;logs --- This checks the command logs. Useful for finding people who abuse admin
;chatlogs --- This shows what people have been saying in the chat
;fly --- Self explanitory
;noclip ---- allows you to go through walls to inaccessable places
;bring --- Brings the player of choice to you ||DO NOT USE ON HRS SHRS||
;to --- Brings you to the selected player
;servermessage --- Sends a message to the server
;serverhint --- sends and anonomous message to the server
;countdown --- Starts a countdown (Used for shifts) the countdown is mesured in seconds eg. 60 seconds is a minute
;rank ranks people lower than you into teams

These are not all the commands but are the commands most used
